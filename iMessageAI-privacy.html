PRIVACY POLICY FOR MESSAGEAI

Last updated: February 11, 2026 Ross Brancati ("we," "our," or "us") operates the messageAI iMessage application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address through Firebase Authentication. This is used solely for account creation, login, and linking your subscription data.

Usage Data: We store your subscription tier, message count, and image generation count in Google Firebase Firestore to enforce monthly usage limits. This data is linked to your account and is not shared with third parties.

User-Generated Content: When you use the chat or image generation features, your text prompts and uploaded images are sent to the Google Gemini API for processing. This content is transmitted in real time and is not stored on our servers. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for information on how Google handles data processed through the Gemini API.

Analytics and Crash Reporting: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous usage statistics and crash reports. This helps us understand how the App is used and improve its stability. Analytics data includes events such as messages sent, images generated, and subscription purchases. Crash reports may include device type, operating system version, and anonymized stack traces. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics or crash data.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION:

- To provide and maintain the App's functionality

- To manage your account and subscription

- To enforce usage limits based on your subscription tier

- To process your chat messages and image generation requests through the Google Gemini API

- To improve the App through anonymous analytics

- To diagnose and fix crashes and technical issues

DATA SHARING: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with the following service providers, solely for the purposes described above:

- Google Firebase (authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting)

- Google Gemini API (AI text and image processing)

- Apple (in-app purchase and subscription management)

These service providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

DATA RETENTION: Your account information and subscription data are retained as long as your account is active. Chat messages and image prompts are not stored on our servers and exist only during the active API request. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below.

DATA SECURITY: We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules that restrict data access to authenticated users, and server-side API key management.

HILDREN'S PRIVACY: The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it.

YOUR RIGHTS You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you

- Request correction of inaccurate data

- Request deletion of your data

- Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.

CONTACT US: If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ross.brancati@gmail.com
