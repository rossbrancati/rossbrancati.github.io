iMESSAGEAI SUPPORT

Last updated: February 26, 2026

iMessageAI allows you to interact with AI directly in the iMessage app. It adds a layer of conveince for using AI when you are messaging a friend, colleague, or family member.

HOW TO USE:

1. Download the iOS app from the App Store
2. Open iMessage and click the plus sign to the left of the text box to open the iMessage app drawer
3. Sign up or sign in and select a plan (Free, Premium, or Pro)
4. Select the chat or image generation feature. Send your prompt and wait for a response
5. Share the response directly in iMessage.
